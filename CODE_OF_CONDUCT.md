# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in KeiOS a respectful and welcoming experience for everyone,
regardless of background, identity, experience level, or perspective.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that helps create a positive community include:

- Being kind, patient, and constructive.
- Respecting different viewpoints and technical preferences.
- Giving feedback that focuses on the work, not the person.
- Accepting responsibility, apologizing when needed, and learning from mistakes.
- Helping keep discussions useful for maintainers and contributors.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, insults, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Trolling, sustained disruption, or deliberately off-topic arguments.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Any conduct that would reasonably make others feel unsafe or unwelcome.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing acceptable behavior. Maintainers
may remove comments, close issues or pull requests, block users, or take other appropriate action
when behavior harms the project community.

If you see or experience unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers privately. Reports will be
reviewed with care and handled as confidentially as practical.
